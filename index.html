S.L.U.G. Project

The Spectacular Landmark Understanding and Guidance (S.L.U.G. Program) is a website dedicated to showing off cool landmarks on the UC Santa Cruz campus. Users will be able to immerse themselves in a self-directed UC Santa Cruz tour and learn about cool nature/buildings to go to. People may be encouraged to go outside more and check out this pretty campus!
